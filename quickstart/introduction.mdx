## Overview
The Martian router system helps you optimize your LLM usage by intelligently routing requests to the most appropriate model based on your requirements. By automatically selecting the optimal model for each request, Martian helps you:
- Reduce costs while maintaining quality
- Improve response quality within your budget
- Automatically optimize based on feedback
