## Overview

The Martian router system helps you optimize your LLM usage by intelligently routing requests to the most appropriate model based on your requirements. The system consists of two main components:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Routers" icon="route">
    Direct each prompt to the optimal LLM based on cost or quality constraints
  </Card>
  <Card title="Judges" icon="gavel">
    Define and measure the quality metrics that routers optimize for
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intelligent Routing">
    Automatically select the best model based on your constraints and requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cost Optimization">
    Set cost constraints to get the best possible quality within your budget.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quality Control">
    Define quality metrics and ensure responses meet your standards.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Continuous Improvement">
    Learn from feedback to make better routing decisions over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
